Site Design

Various websites, microsites and other web-based pieces that I have designed and coded. These sites range from single page static sites to fully-blown dynamic sites with custom Wordpress themes.

Metro Music Makers

Metro Music Makers was looking for a full redesign of their website, which contained tons of information, a blog, and their crucial registration paths for new students. I provided a fresh, new design, better information architecture, and integrated their entire site into Wordpress for easier deployment of content.

Crossfit North Alpharetta

Crossfit North Alpharetta had only been using a Wordpress.com blog to get its WODs to their member base for quite a while. They decided they wanted to launch a full web presence, so I took the Wordpress base, made them a custom theme, helped them craft an easy to navigate site with properly organized information.

Teaching Little Children

TLC was a local, fast growing dance studio that had an extremely rudimentary website. Deborah wanted to convey a cheery, princess-like feel for the aesthetic. I designed and built a custom Wordpress theme with multiple unique pages/elements. I also set them up with being able to collect payments on their website, as they were only doing it over the phone.
